Accet Tracking


Product overview



Some of the features supported by our system include: As the world has shrunk to become one small global village, so have the organization's territories become boundaryless. Organizations have broken the physical boundaries on the markets they address and so has their assets spread all over. The ease to move assets from one point to another causes logistical nightmares to many organizations.

How would inventory managers feel if they knew on a single click of a button they can see real-time location of an asset regardless of the geographical location? At IQ-Things, we tag all your valuable assets with our smart sensors that enable us to know where your asset is whenever you want.

Our Asset Tracking system will enable you to:

  • Know where your assets are
  • Know key environmental conditions around your asset such temperature, humidity, light intensity and other conditions as you may define
  • Know when your asset is moving (if it's not meant to be in motion)
  • Automatically update your inventory management system when assets move out of your warehouse
