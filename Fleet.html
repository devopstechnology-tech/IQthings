Fleet Management


Product overview



The Fleet Management system uses satellite technology to render real-time vehicle location and record historical data. Vehicle tracking units can be installed in each of your vehicles. Our highly trained technicians install these units for optimum performance according to your vehicleâ€™s design & specifications.
Our fleet tracking solution will present you key telematic data from your vehicles on a real-time basis.
Some of the features supported by our system include:

  • GPS Location (2M Accuracy)
  • Speed
  • Driver behaviour (Harsh Acceleration, Emergency braking, dangerous corner negotiations, etc)
  • Direction (While in motion)
  • Engine status (on/off)
  • Impact (on accident)
  • Millage (counter)
  • Tamper detection (incase tracker is disconnected)
  • Geofencing capability
  • Remote engine switch on/off
  • Driver identification
  • Fuel consumption

Applications


Our Fleet tracking solution is applicable in the following areas:

  • Motor bike (Boda Boda) tracking
  • Heavy Commercial Trucks Tracking
  • Personal Vehicle Tracking
