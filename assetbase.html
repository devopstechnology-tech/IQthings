Asset Management



Overview



Today many industries such as construction, mining and agriculture often rent equipment such as generators, cranes, tractors and elevators. It is hard to manage, secure and calculate the working hours manually. All those companies require tools and technology to help them to manage, secure and calculate working hours electronically and remotely.

iQ-Things offers a solution which enables them to monitor how equipment is used and adherence to contractual obligations. The other side of the coin is that it protects the renter, from liability stemming from contractual abuse, infringement and careless use of equipment.
