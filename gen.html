Generator Monitoring


Product overview



Our Generator Monitoring solution is a combination of various sensors that ensure all key data/information is captured on a real-time basis, analyzed and reported in a central database.

This data is stored in a cloud environment and is accessible by the client in a friendly graphical interface for easy understanding.

Our solution is designed for service providers, Telcos, and enterprise customers to enhance resources management and optimizing their usage.



Value proposition

Our solution offers the following benefits:

Real-time information

You will have access to real-time performance of your generators in the field. Such information includes, but not limited to:

  • Engine status (On and Off)
  • Engine run time per given time (chose per hour/day/week/month/year)

  • Realtime GPS location
  • Fueling patterns, and many more
Scheduled automated reports

Get automatic reports on performance of your generators. We have over 20 templates to customize your report from. Such reports include:

  • Fuel consumption report
  • Engine run-time report
  • Maintenance report
  • Fueling pattern report
Predictive/Preventive alerts

Get warnings in advance to prevent major faults of your equipment. This solution will provide you with alerts such as:

  • Upcoming Maintenance alerts
  • Suspected fuel theft alerts
  • Abnormal temperature alerts

With the above and other features supported by our solution, our customers will be able to make quick and informed decisions since all will be based on facts on the ground and on a real-time basis.


Applications


Our Generator monitoring solution is applicable in all types of Diesel engine powered generators.
