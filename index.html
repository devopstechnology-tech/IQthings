Services

iQ-Things utilizes the Internet of Things (IoT) and Machine Learnings (ML) technologies to provide the most efficient and cost effective solutions to our customers.
Today's world demands business decisions to be made using real time data with minimum (if any) error margin. iQ-Things Limited offers the following solutions


Fleet Tracking

Our fleet tracking solution will present you key telematic data from your vehicles on a real-time basis.




Generator Monitoring

Our Generator Monitoring solution is a combination of various sensors that ensure all key data/information is captured on a real-time basis, analyzed and reported in a central database.

Asset Tracking

At IQ-Things, we tag all your valuable assets with our smart sensors that enable us to know where your asset is whenever you want.
